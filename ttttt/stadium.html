SPOTIFY CAMP NOU

Spotify Camp Nou is the iconic home stadium of FC Barcelona, located in Barcelona, Spain. It's the largest stadium in Europe, boasting a capacity of 99,354 before renovations. The stadium, originally named Estadi del FC Barcelona, was inaugurated in 1957 and became popularly known as Camp Nou (meaning "new field"). In 2022, it was renamed Spotify Camp Nou due to a sponsorship deal. Currently, the stadium is undergoing extensive renovations and is expected to have an increased capacity of 105,000 upon completion.
